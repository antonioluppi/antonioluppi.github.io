About me

My name is Antonio Carlos Luppi Junior, AKA Luppi. I am almost graduating in Electrical Engineering at the Federal University of Santa Catarina.
A commited embedded systems designer who also likes the fun, ludic, democratic and inspiring side of technology.

Punctuality, and Simplicity are essential qualities for a sucessful project. That's why I tend to work in quick interactions, until the jobs is good enough for production. When possible, I also like to bring some creative and inovative elements to my solutions, pushing regular solutions to a more investigative level

Talking about leisure time, I'm a very active member of Tarrafa HackerClube, a local Hackerspace founded in 2012 in Florianópolis, doing some basic and intermediate electronics workshops, promoting some reverse engineering and hardware hacking sessions, and evangelizing the Hacker movement for the people.

Engineering services

Analog, Digital and simple power electronics hardware design and prototyping; PCB design & revision under Design for Test, Manufacturing and Assembly (DFT/DTMA) guidelines;

Development of test fixtures and scripts for manufacturing testing, boundary scan verification, functional testing, In-field BIST and metrics, and simple database design for production and field returns logging

Firmware and application programming of microcontroled systems (Atmega328, mcp430, ARM Cortex-M4) and embedded linux;
